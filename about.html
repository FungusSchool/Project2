Coca-Cola Classic

Coca ‑ Cola has a long history and has been a popular drink among people around the world since it was created in 1886 by the pharmacist Dr. John Pemberton in Atlanta, USA. Since then, the drink has been sold as a thirst quencher for everyone and 98 percent of the world's population knows the brand. Coca ‑ Cola is today found in more than 200 countries and was launched in Sweden in 1953.

Coca-Cola Light

Coca ‑ Cola light was launched in Sweden in 1983 and then became the first Coca ‑ Cola variant since 1886. Coca ‑ Cola light is a sugar-free and calorie-free soft drink. The product is developed to suit those who like a really good drink but who want it without added sugar. To clarify the lighter cola taste, Coca ‑ Cola light from the autumn of 2018 is called Coca ‑ Cola light taste.

Coca-Cola Zero

Coca-Cola Zero was introduced around the world in 2006 and quickly became the largest sugar-free beverage on the market. In April 2007, Coca ‑ Cola zero sugar was launched with a new recipe to be as similar to Coca ‑ Cola Original Taste as possible. The idea is to offer consumers the opportunity to choose Coca-Cola, with or without sugar.

Coca-Cola Energy

In 2019, Coca ‑ Cola Energy was launched - an energy drink with a taste of Coca ‑ Cola. The drink contains caffeine from natural sources, guarana extract and vitamin B and is free of taurine. The product comes in two variants, one with sugar and one sugar-free.
